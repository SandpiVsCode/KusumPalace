Travel Desk

Kathmandu

Kathmandu, Nepal's capital is a vally surrounded by hills and mountains is also known as city of temples. A part of Pashupatinath and Boudhanath there are magestic places to explore around. One should not miss nightlife and clubs of city.

Places to visit: Kathmandu Durbar Square, Patan Durbar Square, Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Thamel, Narayanhiti Royal Museum, Chandragiri Hills, Nagarkot

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace - 200 kms

Kalinchowk

Kalinchowk is one of the famous easy tour destination from Kathmandu valley. This tour is the blend of the nature and culture as well as perfect location for hang out with friends and relatives. Kalinchowk Bhagwati Temple is a Hindu shrine in Dolkha District of Nepal.

Places to visit : Palanchowk Bhagwati Mandir, Kalinchowk Bhagwati Mandir, Rolwaling Range & Tamakoshi Hydro-power view from Champuja Hariyali Park, Snow activities (During Snowfall), 4WD Off-Road Drive Experience

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 225 kms

Janakpur

Birthplace of Goddess Sita and the place of her marriage with Load Ram is regarded as religious place for Hindus. Also known as the City of Ponds, Janakpur boasts of more than seventy ponds that dot its verdant landscape. It also represents Mithila culture and arts and it's significance from the time of Ramayana.

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 50 kms

Lumbini

Siddhartha Gautama, the Lord Buddha, was born in 623 B.C. in the famous gardens of Lumbini, which soon became a place of pilgrimage. Among the pilgrims was the Indian emperor Ashoka, who erected one of his commemorative pillars there. The site is now being developed as a Buddhist pilgrimage centre, where the archaeological remains associated with the birth of the Lord Buddha form a central feature.

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 337 kms

Manokamana

Manakamana Devi temple is a highly sacred place of the Hindu Goddess Bhagwati, an incarnation of Parvati. The name Manakamana is derived from two words, Mana, meaning heart and Kamana, meaning wish. It is believed that the Goddess Bhagwati rewards those who make the pilgrimage to her shrine by granting their wishes. Cable car and sightseeing are the attractions around the temple.

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 265 kms

Bungee Jumping

Bungee Jumping in Nepal is one of ose activities that have gained wide popularity in the last few years at quite a fast rate. Nepal offers quite a lot of great views so it is perfect for Bungee Jumping here.

In Nepal, you can bungee jump in two places - The Last Resort (Bhote Koshi) Gorge and Hemja, Pokhara.

Chitwan

Chitwan is popular for Jungle activities, Pilgrimages tours, Water activities and Cultural variations. Chitwan National Park is popular for Jungle activities like: Jungle Safari, Elephant Ride, Jeep Drive and Safari, Bird Watching, it is also home of One Horned Rhino. Chitwan district is also home of Indigenous Tharu people and we can watch and understated their culture and living style very closely.

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 200 kms

Nandi Tal

Nadhi Tal is the popular tourist destination for surrounding areas of Sarlahi (Nepal) and Bihar (India). It has been go to destination for families and friends in weekend. Boating and sceneries are the attractions around the Tal.

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 12 kms

Bharat Tal

Baharat Lake, man made lake, located in Bagmati Municipality of Sarlahi District, is gaining more national & indian tourist attractions in recent days. This lake covers an area of 121 bighas, which has depth of 35 feet. Trip to the lake can be done either in motor boats or jet boats, which are lined by Nepali national flags.

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 40 kms, 60 Mins Ride

Pokhara

In the mountain ranges of Mount. Annapurna lies most popular tourist destination of Nepal. You can take an adventure trip to this city and also enjoy sightseeing at this place. Pokhara has many places to see and offers numerous outdoor activities including the Everest basecamp trek. Places to visit : Phewa lake, Davis Fall, Sarangkot Mountain, Adventure sports in Pokhara, Annapurna Circuit

Distance from Hotel Kusum Palace : 320 kms
